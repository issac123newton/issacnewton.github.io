HTML Lists

Home HTML Tables HTML Lists

HTML lists are used to group related items together. There are three main types of lists:

1. Unordered Lists (<ul>)

Used for items where the order doesn't matter (bulleted lists):

  • Sports
  • Reading
  • camera
  • Painting

2. Ordered Lists (<ol>)

Used when items need to be in a specific order (numbered lists):

  1. Wake up at 7:00 AM
  2. Have breakfast
  3. Attend online classes
  4. Practice hobby for 1 hour
  5. Complete homework

3. Description Lists (<dl>)

Used for terms and their descriptions:

Photography
The art of capturing light with a camera to create images
Basketball
A team sport where two teams compete to score points by shooting a ball through a hoop
Painting
The practice of applying paint to a surface to create artwork

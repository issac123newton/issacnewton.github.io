Hobbies are activities that students enjoy doing in their free time. They help reduce stress, develop new skills, and provide a creative outlet. Popular hobbies among students include sports, reading, painting, coding, playing musical instruments, and photography.

Engaging in hobbies can improve mental health, boost confidence, and even help with academic performance by teaching time management and discipline. Many students find that their hobbies complement their studies and help them develop well-rounded personalities.
